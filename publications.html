A Light Weight Cryptographic Solution for 6LoWPAN Protocol Stack

S. Khairnar, G. Bansod & V. Dahiphale.
In *Intelligent Computing (SAI 2018),* Advances in Intelligent Systems and Computing, Vol. 857, pp. 977–994.
First Online: 02 Nov 2018. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
This work proposes a lightweight cipher (LiCi-2) tailored for resource-constrained IoT environments (6LoWPAN). The design emphasizes minimal memory footprint (1856 bytes flash, 1272 bytes RAM), low power consumption (~25 mW vs 38 mW for PRESENT), and hardware implementation of ~1051 gate equivalents. The paper includes analysis against linear, differential, biclique, and avalanche attacks, and compares performance with other lightweight ciphers. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
